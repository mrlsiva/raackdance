Job Title: Video Editor

Join Raack Dance Academy as a skilled Video Editor! Create captivating and visually stunning videos that showcase the artistry of dance. Collaborate with our creative team to produce engaging content, ensuring seamless integration of footage, music, and graphics. Stay updated with industry trends and bring your creative flair to enhance our video storytelling.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience in video editing.
  • Proficiency in industry-standard editing software.
  • Strong understanding of video production techniques.
  • Excellent time management and collaboration skills.
  • Passion for dance and artistic storytelling.

Ready to make an impact through your video editing skills? Apply now with your resume, portfolio/showreel, and a brief cover letter sharing your interest in joining Raack Dance Academy.
