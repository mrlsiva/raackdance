Join Raack Dance Academy as a skilled Instructor! Teach, provide feedback, and inspire a love for dance. You play a key role in helping students develop their skills and passion for dance. Send us your dance videos, attend our auditions, get selected in these processes, Rest would be history.

Requirements:

  • Passion for Dance:
    Enthusiasm for dance and a genuine desire to share your knowledge and skills with others.
  • Dance Proficiency:
    Proficient in the specific dance style(s) you will be teaching (e.g hip-hop, house, contemporary, etc.).
    Demonstrated skill in choreography and dance technique.
  • Teaching Experience:
    Prior experience as a dance instructor is preferred but not mandatory for entry-level positions.
  • Communication Skills:
    Clear and effective verbal communication to instruct and demonstrate dance moves.
    Ability to provide constructive feedback to students.
  • Patience and Adaptability:
    Ability to work with students of varying ages, skill levels, and learning styles.
    Flexibility to adapt teaching methods to meet individual student needs.
  • Safety Awareness:
    Knowledge of proper warm-up and stretching techniques to prevent injuries.
    Ability to create a safe and supportive learning environment.
  • Leadership and Organization:
    Strong leadership skills to guide and motivate students during classes and rehearsals.
    Organizational skills to plan and manage class schedules and performances.
  • Professionalism:
    Punctuality, reliability, and a professional demeanor when interacting with students, parents, and colleagues.
  • Continuous Learning:
    Willingness to stay updated on current trends, techniques, and best practices in dance instruction through training, workshops, and professional development opportunities.

Branch Manager
Full-time
Apply Details

Job Title: Branch Manager

A Branch Manager at a dance studio oversees day-to-day operations, manages staff, schedules classes, and ensures customer satisfaction. You are responsible for the smooth functioning of the branch, from financial management to maintaining a safe and welcoming environment for students.

Requirements:

  • Operations Management:
    Oversee day-to-day operations of the dance studio branch.
    Ensure smooth functioning of classes, rehearsals, and events.
  • Staff Supervision:
    Recruit, train, and manage dance instructors and support staff.
    Provide guidance and support to staff members.
  • Scheduling and Coordination:
    Develop and maintain class schedules, ensuring optimal utilization of studio space and resources.
    Coordinate with instructors to ensure classes run smoothly and on schedule.
  • Customer Relations:
    Interact with parents, students, and clients to address inquiries, concerns, and feedback.
    Ensure a high level of customer satisfaction and resolve any issues promptly.
  • Financial Management:
    Monitor and manage the branch budget, including revenue, expenses, and profitability.
    Implement pricing strategies and promotions to meet revenue goals.
  • Marketing and Promotion:
    Work with the marketing team to develop and execute marketing strategies to attract new students and retain existing ones.
    Organize and promote events, workshops, and performances.
  • Facility Maintenance and Safety:
    Ensure the cleanliness, safety, and maintenance of the studio space.
    Implement safety protocols and emergency procedures.
  • Curriculum Development:
    Collaborate with dance instructors to develop and refine class curriculum.
    Ensure that classes align with the studio's overall teaching philosophy and standards.
  • Administrative Tasks:
    Handle administrative duties such as scheduling, payroll, and record-keeping.
    Maintain accurate and up-to-date student and staff records.
  • Reporting and Communication:
    Provide regular reports to the management team on branch performance, including financial metrics, enrollment numbers, and customer feedback.
    Communicate effectively with the head office and other branches.

Ready to make an impact through your managerial skills? Apply now with your resume and a brief cover letter sharing your interest in joining Raack Dance Academy.
