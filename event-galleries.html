Annual
Events

Get ready for a dazzling showcase of
Talent & Artistry

Get ready to immerse yourself in the exciting world of dance through our exhilarating annual events. We proudly present two remarkable events that showcase the immense talent and creativity of our students

First Step (Recital)

Join us every February for our much-awaited annual gala, First Step. This spectacular showcase brings together over 2000 talented students who take their first step onto the stage to express their unique style of dance. With a grand setup and mesmerizing performances, First Step is a true celebration of the passion and dedication that defines Raack Dance Academy.

It's a momentous event that allows our students to experience the joy and thrill of performing, leaving them with memories to cherish for a lifetime.

Happy Feet
(In House Dance Contest)

At Raack Dance Academy, we believe in nurturing the individuality and creative expression of each child. Happy Feet, our In-House Dance Contest held every three months, celebrates the unique choreography created by our students themselves. This event encourages our young dancers to embrace resilience, as they learn to both celebrate victories and embrace failures as part of their journey.

The contest not only showcases their exceptional talent but also provides an opportunity for them to grow and develop as confident performers. As an added motivation, top-performing students are awarded scholarships and prizes to encourage their continued growth.
