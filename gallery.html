Photo Galleries

We invite you to immerse yourself in the vibrant world of dance through captivating visuals that showcase the beauty, grace, and energy of our talented dancers. Browse through our carefully curated photo galleries and witness the artistry that unfolds on our dance floors.

First Step (2023)

The First Step, as the name defines is our annual gala showcasing the immense talents of our extraordinary students. Happening every February, more than 2000 students participate each year to express their style of dance on stage.

Happy Feet (2022)

The feet of our students cannot step back without creating its own moves after all the love it has received via dancing. Happy Feet, the latest addition to the RAACK events celebrates the individuality of each child through their own choreography in our In-House Dance Contest.

Just Dance it! (2022)

Let loose, groove, and express yourself with our Just Dance it! program. This freestyle dancing course is all about letting the music guide your movements, allowing you to connect with the rhythm, explore your individual style, and experience the joy of dance.
