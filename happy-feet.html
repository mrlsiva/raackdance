Why "Happy Feet"?

Fostering Resilience: Through the ups and downs of preparation, students will learn to persist, adapt, and overcome obstacles, thereby building a strong foundation of resilience that extends beyond the dance floor.

Embracing Sportsmanship: Competitions provide an opportunity to celebrate the achievements of all participants. Win or lose, each dancer will learn the importance of camaraderie, mutual respect, and the joy of celebrating one another's successes.

Cultivating Confidence: Stepping onto a stage, under the spotlight, is a powerful confidence-building experience. "Happy Feet" encourages every participant to embrace their uniqueness and perform with courage and self-assurance.

Competition Categories:
  • Little Legends (Ages 03-06)
  • RDEP Level 1 (Ages 07-12)
  • RDEP Level 2 (Ages 07-12)
  • Choreo Lab (Ages 07-12)
  • Choreo Lab (Ages 13+)
  • Film Covers (Ages 07-12)
  • Film Covers (Ages 13+)
Judging Criteria:

Our esteemed panel of judges will evaluate performances based on:

  • Technique and Execution
  • Artistic Interpretation
  • Stage Presence and Confidence
  • Originality and Creativity
Prizes:
  • Trophies for Winners
  • Certificates of Participation for All

Get ready to unleash your dancing prowess and be part of a day filled with rhythm, passion, and pure dance magic. Let's make "Happy Feet" a celebration of talent, growth, and community!
